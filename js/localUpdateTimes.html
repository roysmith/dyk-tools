Template documentation

This template shows when Did You Know updates are scheduled to appear on the Main Page. It automatically adjusts for Daylight Savings Time according to the schedule below. Using the template with no parameters gives the table above. The template can display a custom time zone with the customshift parameter. For example, the code below shows the update times in Zimbabwe. The customshift parameter must have a plus or minus sign in front of the number of hours the time zone is offset from UTC. If you want UTC times, enter "+0 hours" or "-0 hours". 

{{Template:Did you know/Queue/LocalUpdateTimes |customname=Zimbabwe |customshift=+2 hours}}


Schedule for Daylight Savings Time this year (time is offset by +1 hour compared to non-DST):

  • New York: March 9 08:00 UTC – November 2 07:00 UTC
  • London: March 30 01:00 UTC – October 26 01:00 UTC
  • Sydney: October 5 16:00 UTC – April 6 16:00 UTC
  • New Delhi and Tokyo do not observe Daylight Savings Time
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (create | mirror) and testcases (create) pages.
